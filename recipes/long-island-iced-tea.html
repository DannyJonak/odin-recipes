Long Island Iced Tea

Home

Description

Big drink!

Ingredients

  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1 oz rum
  • 1 oz gin
  • 1 oz tequila
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 2.5 oz sweet and sour mix
  • Splash Cola

Steps

  1. Add ice to tall glass.
  2. Pour in vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sweet and sour mix.
  3. Top with a splash of cola.
