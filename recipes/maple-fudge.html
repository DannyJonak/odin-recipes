Maple Fudge

Home

Description

You know what fudge is.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup lightly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Line 8in square pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a heavy saucepan, bring all ingredients except vanilla to a boil, and stir until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Let simmer without stirring until candy thermometer placed in centre reads 240℉.
  4. Remove from heat and add vanilla without stirring.
  5. Place the pan in a water bath and let cool without stirring until the thermometer reads 110℉.
  6. Remove pan from water and beat the mixture until it thickens and loses its gloss.
  7. Immediately pour into the prepared pan and let cool for about 1 hour.
